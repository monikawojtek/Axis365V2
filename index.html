Our Services

PRIMARY CARE CENTRES

Since the early 2000s, we have advised and assisted clients in the delivery of Primary Care Centres (PCCs). Highly experienced in the design, build, financing and operation of PCCs, we work for various stakeholders in the PCC delivery process. In addition to developing PCCs ourselves, we provide the following services to the Primary Care sector:

  • Project Management
  • Design, Build, Finance and Operate
  • Tenant Representative
  • Equity Funding
  • Facilities Management
  • Quality Assurance

MAINTENANCE MANAGEMENT AND ASSET CARE

We are strong advocates of the Lean Six Sigma approach to Risk Management in Maintenance and Asset Care. We have extensive experience working with customers in improving their performance and outputs. Many of our customers operate in the regulated sectors – Health Care, Medical Devices, Food and Pharmaceutical. Effective and efficient asset reliability is our mantra and we provide the following services to reliability-focused customers:

  • Master / Improvement Planning
  • Preventive Maintenance Optimisation / FMEA
  • Planning and Scheduling
  • Value Stream Mapping
  • Asset Criticality Ranking
  • CMMS / EAMS / CAFM
  • Auditing
